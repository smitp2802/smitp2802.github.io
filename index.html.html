01 —

Kawasaki Apex

Concept Sneaker Design

Design Intent

Translating the aggressive, aerodynamic, and performance-oriented design language of Kawasaki superbikes into a wearable product — capturing not just the visual identity but also the emotional intensity and speed-driven character of the brand.

Key Inspiration

  • Sharp cuts and angular surface transitions
  • Forward-leaning stance suggesting motion and acceleration
  • High-contrast black and green color palette
  • Visible mechanical elements expressing raw power

Design Translation

  • Sculpted upper with dynamic surface flow
  • Layered construction mimicking bike fairings
  • Tension lines conveying motion even when static
  • Unified sole form enhancing speed perception

A performance-driven sneaker capturing velocity, precision, and mechanical aggression — transforming automotive DNA into a bold wearable form.

02 —

Monotick

Minimal Timepiece Concept

Design Intent

Redefining the perception of time by shifting focus from continuous measurement to a single meaningful moment — reducing time to its most essential and experiential form.

Design Language

  • A single dominant hand as the focal point
  • Clean, distraction-free dial
  • Subtle layering creates depth without complexity
  • Geometry driven by precision and clarity

Interaction Philosophy

  • Encourages mindfulness and awareness of time
  • Reduces cognitive overload caused by constant tracking
  • Converts timekeeping into a sensory experience

Shifts from measuring time to experiencing time — representing clarity, focus, and intentional living.

03 —

Frogelo

Child-Centric Binocular

Problem Statement

Traditional binoculars are intimidating and not child-friendly, with poor ergonomic adaptation for small hands and a lack of emotional connection that reduces engagement and usage.

Key Insight

Children are more likely to engage with products that feel like characters rather than tools.

Design Approach

  • Frog-inspired form for emotional attachment
  • Soft geometry and approachable aesthetics
  • Ergonomically optimized for small hands
  • Simple and intuitive interaction

Transforms from a technical device to a playful companion — significantly enhancing engagement, usability, and learning behavior in children.

04 —

Compact Sling Portfolio

Transformable Carry System

Design Intent

A portable and ergonomic solution that not only carries design sheets safely but also transforms into a functional workspace — bridging the gap between carrying and creating.

Key Features

  • Foldable A2 format → converts to flat working mat
  • Tear-drop form for better weight distribution
  • Internal elastic loops for tools
  • Dedicated storage for accessories

Material Strategy

  • Outer: PU / Canvas — durability + aesthetics
  • Middle: EVA Foam — impact absorption
  • Core: PP Sheet — rigidity without weight
  • Inner Lining: Taffeta — smooth surface for sheets

A multi-functional design tool enhancing mobility and organization — bridging the gap between carrying and creating.
